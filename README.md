## Hi, I'm Corey

I build AI agent tooling, developer productivity workflows, and practical apps for desktop and web. I like turning small, focused ideas into useful products, then tightening the details until they feel simple to use.

Right now, most of my work is around AI agents, automation, and tools that help people move faster without losing control.
